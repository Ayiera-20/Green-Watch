About Us

About Us

GreenWatch is a platform dedicated to transforming waste management in Nairobi. We believe in a collaborative
approach, empowering communities to take charge and contribute to a greener city. Our platform connects
residents with recycling centers, offers educational resources, and facilitates community-driven initiatives, working towards a sustainable future.

Community Engagement

We connect residents with each other and with resources to tackle waste issues.

Recycling Solutions

Sustainable Solutions

The Problem We Address

Nairobi faces significant waste management challenges that impact the health and well-being of its residents.

Illegal dumping

Illegal dumping is prevalent in many areas, contributing to environmental pollution and health hazards.

Inadequate Services

Limited municipal waste collection services lead to uncollected garbage and create sanitation problems.

Limited Recycling

Lack of accessible recycling options leads to more waste ending up in landfills, exacerbating waste management challenges.
